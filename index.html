Werewolf

Which configuration do you want to play?



Configuration
Villager: 4 Villagers
God: Guardian, Seer, Witch, Hunter
Werewolf: 3 Wolves, 1 Hunterwolf

Winning Condition
Werewolves: All Villagers are dead, or all Gods are dead.
Humans (Villagers and Gods): All Werewolves are dead.

Character Intro
Villager Human. The villager does not awake at night.
Guardian God. The Guardian awakes first at night, selects one player at night, and this player will not be killed by the Werewolves that night.
The Guardian is allowed to choose not protecting anyone at night.
The Guardian cannot protect the same player at two consecutive nights.
Witch God. The Witch awakes after the Werewolves.
The Witch has one elixir and one poison. The elixir is used to save a Player from Werewolves, and the poison kills a Player.
The Witch knows who is killed by the Werewolves if and only if the elixir is not used, at which situation the Witch chooses either use or not use the elixir to save the player.
The Witch can choose to use up to one potion each night.
If a player is guarded by the Guardian and saved by the Witch, then the player is still dead.
Seer God. The Seer awakes at night, selects one player at night, and the Seer will know whether this player is a werewolf or not.
Hunter God. When declared death, the Hunter can use the gun to shoot one player to death.
The Hunter cannot shoot anyone if being poisoned by the Witch.
Every night the Hunter awakes to see the shot status:
up: can shoot if dead
down: cannot shoot if dead
Wolf Werewolf. All werewolves awake at the same time and discuss together, choose a player to kill.
Hunterwolf Werewolf. As the name shows, a Hunterwolf is the hunter of Werewolves.
When declared death, the Hunterwolf can use the gun to shoot one player to death.
The Hunterwolf cannot shoot anyone if being poisoned by the Witch.
Every night the Hunterwolf awakes to see the shot status:
up: can shoot if dead
down: cannot shoot if dead
